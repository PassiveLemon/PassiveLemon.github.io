Hi, Lemon here.

I like writing code and programming. I really do. I feel like it ties into my "logically configured" mind. I love the challenge it can offer and the critical thinking I need to come up with solutions that aren't completely awful and make some sense. This kind of makes me think very directly though. I don't care for personal interpretation and, with a mixture of my limited ability to literally visualize things, I am not very creative. Trust me. When it comes to stuff that does not affect me in a major way, I will have issues deciding from personal preference. Ask me what I would like to eat and I would not be able to decide until I look in the fridge and see what I have.

You may have heard of something called Aphantasia. It's a condition where an individual cannot or has great difficulty picturing stuff in their mind. I don't have it but I would think it ties into my limited creativity. You may have also heard what I am about to say but, if you haven't, hear this: Picture an apple. Just an apple. It can be anywhere you want, any color, anything you want. What do you see? Do you see fine details? Stripes? Is it moist with water drops? Does it have a stem? Is it colorful?
